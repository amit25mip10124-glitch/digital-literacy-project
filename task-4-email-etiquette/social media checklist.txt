Social Media Do’s ✅

Think before posting anything online
Use privacy settings to protect personal information
Share useful and positive content
Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely
Use social media for learning and networking

Social Media Don’ts ❌

Don’t share personal details like address or passwords
Don’t post offensive or abusive comments
Don’t spread fake news or unverified information
Don’t waste too much time scrolling unnecessarily
Don’t post anything that can harm your reputation
